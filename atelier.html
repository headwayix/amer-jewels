The Atelier

Where heritage meets the hand.

Down a lane in the old city, in lamplight and quiet concentration, our craftspeople cut, form and set each piece by hand — the same way it has been done here for generations.

Movement I · The Stone

Chosen by eye, cut by hand

Each rough stone is read for colour, clarity and fire, then ground and polished against the wheel until its character comes alive — one gem at a time.

A single lamp, a steady hand, and hours of patience.
Movement II · The Hand

Formed at the bench

Silver and gold are drawn, filed, soldered and shaped over the flame. Every blossom, bezel and link is worked by hand until the metal is ready to hold its stone.

The Bench

Hammers, files, callipers and pliers — many older than the makers who wield them.

Movement III · The Setting

Every stone, placed by hand

Under the loupe, each gem is sorted, matched and set with tweezers and patience until the piece is whole.

The People

The hands behind every piece

Our workshop is a family of master craftspeople whose skill has been handed down through generations. When you wear Amer Jewels, you carry their artistry with you.

“A piece is finished when it carries a story.”
